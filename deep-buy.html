🔥 Flow-extreme picks single-axis flow convergence — long streak + extreme call dominance

Narrower diagnostic than flow-led: streak ≥10 days, TD-confirmed P/C ≤0.15 (6:1 calls), avg flow ≥+7 over 5 days. Pre-revenue / small-cap names the multi-path composite structurally misses. Halved risk budget reflects the single-axis nature. Backtest: 92% hit / SR 1.37 on 5d.
