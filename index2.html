Methodology

Unified portfolio: Combines Path 1 (lifecycle signals, score 60-79, first detected in 10-day window) and Path 3 (contrarian deep-value, score 60-79, down 40%+ from high). Equal-weight across all surviving tickers.

Path 1: Calibration analysis (742 signals, Feb-Mar 2026) shows Day 3-10 from first detection is the optimal entry window. Score 60-79 outperforms 80+.

Path 3 (experimental): Deep-value plays down 40%+ from 52-week high. Hypothesis under forward validation — not yet proven.

Oracle curation: AI analyst reviews every ticker. Can blocklist (remove) or rerank via score adjustments. Cannot add tickers — rules-based entry only.

Risk controls: $5 minimum price, -10% stop-loss. Market mood gauge (-100 to +100) informs position sizing caution.

Benchmarks: SPY and MAG7 returns computed over identical holding periods (Path 1 entry dates).
